About Us

  • 1995

    Our Humble Beginnings.

    PT. Titian Bahtera Indonesia is a shipping company agency which was first built in 1995 in Batam Riau islands of Indonesia. Our company is engaged in the field of shipping agency and providing labor for companies in the shipping sector, where we currently serve several domestic and international offshore companies.

  • Partnership with PT. Britoil Offshore Singapore.

    Our company started from our partnership with an international company, PT. Britoil Offshore Singapore in 1995 as the only agent partner in Indonesia, and we opened up opportunities for cooperation with other companies both domestically and internationally

Our works

Pt. Titian Bahtera Indonesia as a shipping company we have two main jobs that we do namely As a ship agency partner and also as a company that provides trained and experienced workers to be hired. As one of the pride for our company that we have been recognized as one of the companies to the best agents in Indonesia and now we have qualified workers in their fields.
